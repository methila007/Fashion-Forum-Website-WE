How to Get The Graphic Eyebrow Look

      

 
We all know that a very precise,  graphic eyebrow sculpts and stretches the eyes, 

providing them with character and determination.


Here are 4 steps to follow to achieve the Graphic Brow look with Makeup 

Forever’s 3-in-1 Brow Sculpting Pen.


1. Brush & style the eyebrows.
      

 
2. Structure the eyebrow by drawing clear lines around it.

Press down with the tip to create a clean line.

Extend the tail of the eyebrow slightly to create the desired graphic aspect.
      

 
4. Apply the matte powder to the brow bone to structure

the eyebrow and highlight the eyes.
      

 
5. For a perfectly defined and structured result, apply the light shade

from the Pro Sculpting Palette to the eyebrow contour using brush.
