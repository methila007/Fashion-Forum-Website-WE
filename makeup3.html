4 cool Instagram-famous makeup brands
you need to follow

      

 
 Is there anything Instagram can’t do? From introducing us to new and delightfully weird trends to

 providing baby name inspo, we could honestly spend the rest of our lives just scrolling and double-tapping.


Once upon a time, our favourite thing to do on Instagram was creep our ex-boyfriends, but not anymore.

(#PersonalGrowth2016.) These days, we’re using it to find new makeup brands to check out and obsess

over. When it comes to quality makeup, depending on the look you were going for, your choices used to 

be limited to a handful of well-known brands that you could choose from. Now, it’s easier than ever to

find brands that are killing it on Instagram, check out their product swatches and watch how-to videos.

Some of them have amassed huge followings (upwards of a million!) so all those people can’t be wrong.


Our favourite thing about these makeup brands is how involved they are. From posting countless swatches

comparing shades to other brand shades (upon the request of readers) to responding to all comments, 

these pages are the place to go for makeup junkies with questions.

Here are some of our favourite Insta-famous makeup brands.



Melt Cosmetics
      

 
 Started by one of Rihanna’s makeup artists, Lora Arellano and her friend Dana Bomar, 

 and is known for heavily pigmented, bright shades. A few years ago, Rihanna spotted one 

 of Lora’s makeup looks on Instagram and asked her to do her Bride of Mary Jane costume

 makeup, and eventually join her on tour. If Melt Cosmetics’ RiRi approval isn’t enough for 

 you, it also has 1.8 million followers on Instagram..



L.A. Splash Cosmetics
      

 
 Carrying everything from lip gloss to matte lipstick to mascara and nail polish, L.A. Splash 

 Cosmetics is affordable (nail polishes are about $4 and glosses are $3) and has a huge 

 selection so you can experiment with colours you wouldn’t normally wear, without 

 hurting your wallet. The brand has 442,000 followers on Instagram and is quickly 

 growing. Oh, and check out the video above to see just how waterproof these guys are.




ColourPop Cosmetics
      

 
 Hot on the heels of a Kylie Jenner shout out and a collab with Karrueche, the brand has

 been growing steadily for some time now, and it now sits at 1.6 million followers. It’s 

 popular with the Tumblr crowd and is known for the Ultra Matte Lips line, which 

 comes in a huge array of colours (everything from red to nude to blue). At only $6

 (USD) a pop, can you really afford not to try them?




Sugarpill Cosmetics
      

 
 It’s easy to see why Sugarpill Cosmetics has 1.2 million followers. Along with product swatches

 and fan photos, the brand also posts some incredible inspo shots that’ll get you itching to try 

 something new, from glitter lips to glass nails to rhinestone brows. The brand’s site

 (and product packaging) looks like a Katy Perry video, and that just adds to its charm.
