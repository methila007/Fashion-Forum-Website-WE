Best Hair Trends And Styles For Spring/Summer 2016

      

 
Every woman uses her hair as a tool to express herself – while clothes and accessories pretty much 

change their trends every season,  hair isn’t normally something we would think we can change every 

season, but at least every year. A woman can look street-wise in one moment and then quite professional 

in another, she can be cute and sexy. The right hairstyle build up our confidence and picking the right one

for you this summer is crucial.
      

 
 The hottest hair colours to rock this season are: Fiery Red Hair, New Bleach Blonde – 

 the deeper kiss of the sun, Golden Waves, Deep Brunette and Lots of Unnatural Colors!
