See Rihanna’s new campaign as the face of Dior

      

 
Dior’s newest campaign, Secret Garden IV, has just been released and it couldn’t star

a more perfect leading lady. Just days after her show stopping fashion moment at this 

year’s Met Gala, Rihanna takes over as the newest face of Dior‘s Esprit collection, shown

last year in Tokyo.


Shot by Steven Klein, who describes “Rihanna’s mystery and intrigue” as the perfect

backdrop for the campaign, he transports us to a world of darkened enchantment. 

The scene is set in the hauntingly beautiful gardens and chateau of Versailles, where

 the singer (and Dior’s newest must-have, the Diorama bag) takes centre stage.


The house has released 8 stunning shots in addition to two mini teaser-clips of a longer

video to come. The pictures are utter perfection (how could they not be with a duo like 

Klein and RiRi) but the videos are what truly transport you to this world of old and new.
