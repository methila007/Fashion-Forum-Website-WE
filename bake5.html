Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

      

 
 Crisp edges, chewy middles

 
 Ingredients

 
 *1 cup butter, softened 

 *2 eggs         

 *3 cups all-purpose flour*  

 2 teaspoons hot water   
          
 *1/2 teaspoon salt     
             
 *2 cups chocolate chips  
      
 *1 cup chopped walnuts     
 
 *1 cup white sugar 

 *1 teaspoon baking soda 

  *2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  *1 cup packed brown sugar 

 
 
 
 Directions


1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

2.Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. 

Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda

in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and

nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

3.Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.


Tip:Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
