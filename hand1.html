Versace’s “Palazzo Bag” – The IT Bag For Summer 2016

      

 
 Along with its Spring/Summer 2016 Collection, Versace is introducing a completely new  

 bag: the Palazzo Empire. 

 
 The Palazzo Empire is a bag of of supple luxury, crafted in the finest calfskin, or soft  

 exotic leathers such as croc or python. It’s a versatile style that can be carried two  

 ways, either in the hand or across the body with wide coloured strap. Inside are  

 functional pockets and a linen lining.
      

 
  The Palazzo empire is defined by its medusa head, which stands proud in metallic, or painted

  tone-on-tone to match the colour of the bag. As with the Spring/Summer 2016 Show, the

  colours are ready for the urban jungle, in either utilitarian shades or popping colour.
