Get Beyonce’s SuperBowl Makeup Look

      

 
The Diva looked flawless, from her signature bouncy curls to her glowing skin. This year her 

makeup artist aimed for a juicy, honey-kissed look with fresh skin, metallic lids, and a matching lip.


Check out how to achieve that look..
    

 
 For your Skin

Use rose oil  – it hydrates, but it also repairs the skin. It works for oilier complexions too.

Mix it with a bit of water and use it as a mist. It preps your skin for moisturizer or makeup 

primer.Apply a light moisturizer and light layer of liquid matte foundation and  keep it as sheer

as possible.
    

 
 For your Eyes

Use a matte brown powder to sculpt your lids, a shimmery, rust-toned powder 

on the center of the eyes, and a bit of copper powder on top of that.
    

 
 For your Cheeks

Use taupe-toned contouring powder to accentuate the cheekbones, a warm bronzer 

on the apples of her cheeks, and a bit of peachy gold blush.

as possible.
    

 
 For your Lips

Apply a warm nude lipstick and a bit of powder metallic copper pigment.
