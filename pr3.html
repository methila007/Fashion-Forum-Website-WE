Blumarine Pre-Fall 2016

      

 
 Blumarine pre-fall collection for 2016 is so much more masculine than the usual designer’s feminine 

 signature look we are used to seeing.


It’s a known fact that the designer has a thing for roses, lace, embellishments, mink, the color pink. 

She had said that she is a true romantic and will always stay that way. But this season she has given 

modern, urban attitude to the collection, focusing on daywear.
